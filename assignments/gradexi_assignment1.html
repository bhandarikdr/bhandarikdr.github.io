Academic Foundations & Future Vision

Past ➔ Present ➔ Future what · how · why

🔍 Looking back · my foundations

WHAT

Which subjects consumed my energy? Which did I avoid? Was I memorising facts or striving for core concepts? Did I procrastinate or stay consistent? Passive or active strategies? How did I manage distractions?

WHY

What was my core engine? Intrinsic curiosity or external pressure (fear, parents, rank)? How did that motivation affect my mental health, stress, and enjoyment?

chronic cramming? active recall spaced repetition Pomodoro

📝 Where I am now · current reality

WHAT

What is my current academic standing? Which subjects am I strong in? Where do I struggle? What study habits do I have now? How am I managing my time and energy?

WHY

Why am I in this position? What choices led me here? What's working well that I should keep? What needs to change right now?

🚀 Moving forward · Grade 11 journey

WHAT

Which subjects / electives excite me? Which cause anxiety? What measurable milestones and grade targets do I set? How do these connect to my career or university goals?

HOW

Which outdated strategies must I leave behind? Which evidence‑based methods (active recall, spaced repetition, Pomodoro) will I integrate? How will I design my weekly calendar to safeguard health, sleep, and well‑being? What’s my proactive plan for mentorship when I hit friction?

WHY

What is my overarching “Big Why” beyond the diploma? What kind of independent thinker, critical analyst, scholar do I wish to become? How will this vision keep me resilient when motivation fades?
