Q. No. 4. Advanced Report Formatting (Long Document Management)

Task: Create a formal student's final report following the strict formatting requirements below. This task tests your ability to manage long documents with consistent styling, sections, and automated features.

Part A: General Formatting Requirements

  • Font: Times New Roman, Size: 12 pt.
  • Line Spacing: 1.5 line spacing.
  • Margins: 1 Inch on all sides.
  • Paragraphs: Left Alignment and 0.5 Inch Indentation (Except for Abstract/Front Matter sections).
  • Page Numbering: Starting from cover page but not visible on the cover page. Position: Top right.

Part B: Heading Levels & Style Definitions

  • Heading One: Centered, Bold, Capitalize First Letter of major words. Spacing: 0pt before/after.
  • Heading Two: Left Aligned, Bold, Capitalize First Letter of major words. Spacing: 0pt before/after.
  • Heading Three: Left Aligned, Bold and Italicized, Capitalize First Letter of major words. Spacing: 0pt before/after.
  • Heading Four: 0.5" indentation, Left Aligned, Bold, Capitalize First Letter, Period at end. Text follows immediately after title.
  • Heading Five: 0.5" indentation, Left Aligned, Bold and Italicized, Capitalize First Letter, Period at end. Text follows immediately after title.

Part C: Document Structure

Your paper must include the following sections in order:

  • Title Page
  • Letter of Approval
  • Acknowledgements
  • Table of Contents
  • Introduction
  • Chapters (with sub-headings)
  • Conclusions
  • References
  • Appendixes (if applicable)

Part 4 Databases Concept
Database Application Task (MS Access)

Objective: Design a relational database named Einstein_Academy.accdb to manage student and faculty information.
